It's routed through a filter in script.js that opens up gently as she scrolls
further into the page, so it's not just a flat loop — it reacts a little to
her moving through the tracklist.

Starts on her first tap/click/keypress, not on page load — browsers block all
audio from starting with zero real interaction first, no way around that.
The mini-player is the manual play/pause control after that.

Want a different song instead (something that means something to the two of
you specifically, or a recorded voice memo of you singing happy birthday)?

<source> path in index.html if you'd rather use a different filename/format.
