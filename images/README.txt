Drop your own photos in here to fill "The Album Booklet" section.

Expected filenames: photo1.jpg through photo8.jpg
Recommended: portrait/4:5 aspect ratio, at least 800px on the long side, under ~500KB each for fast loading.

If a file isn't here yet, the page shows a graceful placeholder instead of a broken image — it's safe to launch with zero real photos and add them later.

To use different filenames or add/remove photos, edit the PHOTOS array in script.js (near the top, inside the CONFIG object).
